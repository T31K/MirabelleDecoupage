The Studio

Located at Medan Putra Business Centre, the studio is tastefully decorated with decoupaged signboards, modern and minimum furnishing as well as beautifully completed works. Incorporated in 2015, Mirabelle Decoupage Art & Craft provide almost EVERYTHING a decouper would need. Imported decoupage papers, paint, glue, varnish and all types of medium and tools used for decoupage are available at the studio.



Mirabelle Decoupage Art & Craft represent TO-DO Creative Art School (Italy) in conducting Decoupage lessons to hobbyist and authorized to confer TO-DO Teacher’s Certificate in Decoupage to aspiring teachers. ​All TO-DO materials, TO-DO soft paper, Fleur Paint and mediums are sold exclusively at Mirabelle Decoupage studio.



Mirabelle Decoupage Art & Craft is the authorized dealer for SOSPESO TRASPARENTE materials in Malaysia. Regular classes are conducted at Mirabelle Decoupage using original SOSPESO TRASPARENTE techniques and materials.
